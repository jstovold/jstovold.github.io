Record number of people on a Postbox

Extract from the 1988 Guinness book of records:

The record number to pile on top of or hang from a standard double pillar box (oval top of 6 sq ft 0.55 sq m) is 32, all students of Wentworth College, York University in Parliament St, York on 27 Feb 1985.
