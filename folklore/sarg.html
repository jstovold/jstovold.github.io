Scientific Anomalies Research Group

Status: believed true

The Physics department has (although it might not know so) a ``Scientific Anomalies Research Group''. (SARG, which was according to the blurb was ``Founded in 1996 to apply scientific thought and equipment and to assign physical identification to events described as paranormal''. As partial evidence we have the postscript used to create their badges and their headed notepaper. The names and details have been removed to protect the guilty.
