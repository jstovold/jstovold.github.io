I am a member of academic staff in the School of Computing and Communications, Lancaster University Leipzig. My primary research interests are in unconventional computing, emergent intelligence, and swarm robotics. I completed my undergraduate and doctoral studies at the University of York (specifically, as part of YCCSA). I am also a long-time member and fellow of Langwith College.

For more information on my research, please see either the Research or Publications sections.
