The Buddah Statue

The Buddah statue stands near the Language Centre, and a picture of it has appeared in many prospectuses in past years. It has the following plaque in front of it:

The gift of
MRS ELIZABETH COOPER
from the collection of her father
JOHN BOWES MORRELL
November 1983
