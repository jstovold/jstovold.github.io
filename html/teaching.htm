Teaching

N.B. if you are a Lancaster student and are looking for information on your current modules, Moodle is your best source of information.

I have many years' teaching experience, including topics such as non-standard computing, neural networks, multi-agent interaction and games, digital circuit design, and computer systems.

Current Teaching Commitments

At present I am running the first-year Digital Systems module (digital circuit design, computer architectures, assembly language, etc.), along with the third-year Deep Learning module and MSc-level Data Mining module.

If you are interesting in completing your BSc or MSc project with me, please take a look at some of my recent research, and email me to arrange time for a chat. It is much more likely that you will get the project you want if you take the initiative and propose a project yourself.
